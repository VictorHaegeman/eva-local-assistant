# Smart Brief Research

Use this skill when Victor asks for news, market watch, RSS summaries, business signals, AI trends, finance updates, or a morning brief.

Operating pattern:

1. Treat the request as information triage, not generic conversation.
2. Prefer verified local sources already configured for Eva: RSS, Gmail notifications, saved sources, and article fetchers.
3. Read useful article content when available, not only titles.
4. Score every item against Victor's priorities: AI, business, finance, DreamLense, LinkedIn opportunities, risks, product ideas, and concrete decisions.
5. Return only the important parts.

Response shape:

- 3 things to know.
- 1 opportunity.
- 1 risk.
- 1 LinkedIn/content idea.
- 1 useful next action.

Do not invent events, numbers, sources, or trends. If a source is missing or stale, say so briefly and use the best available verified source.
